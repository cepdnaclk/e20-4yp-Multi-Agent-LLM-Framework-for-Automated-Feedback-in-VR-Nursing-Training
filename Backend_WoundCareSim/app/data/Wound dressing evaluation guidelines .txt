WOUND DRESSING PROTOCOL
For Clean Surgical Wounds

PURPOSE

To protect the wound, promote healing, and prevent infection.

STEP-BY-STEP DRESSING PROCEDURE
1. Hand Washing (Before Dressing)

Perform hand washing before touching dressing materials.

2. Verify Sterile Dressing with Staff Nurse (MANDATORY)

Take the sterile dressing packet to the staff nurse.

Ask for verification.

Clearly state:

Dressing type

Expiration date

Package condition (intact / dry)

Wait for approval before opening the packet.

3. Apply Dressing

Ensure the wound is clean and dry.

Place the dressing at the center of the wound.

Ensure the dressing fully covers the wound.

Secure all edges properly.

Label the dressing with date and time.

4. Hand Washing (After Dressing)

Perform hand washing after completing the dressing.

EVALUATION CHECKPOINTS (For Agents)

✔ Hand washing done before and after
✔ Dressing verified with staff nurse
✔ Dressing applied correctly
✔ Dressing secured and labeled

KEY RULES

Staff nurse verification is mandatory.

Dressing must fully cover the wound.

Secure all edges properly.

Hand hygiene before and after is essential.
