WOUND CLEANING PROTOCOL
For Clean Surgical Wounds

PURPOSE

To clean the wound safely, prevent infection, and prepare it for dressing.

STEP-BY-STEP CLEANING PROCEDURE
1. Hand Washing (First Time)

Perform medical hand washing before touching any equipment.

2. Arrange Dressing Trolley

Clean the trolley surface.

Arrange required items neatly on the trolley.

Keep waste items separate.

3. Hand Washing (Second Time)

Perform hand washing again after arranging the trolley.

This is required before patient care.

4. Verify Cleaning Solution with Staff Nurse (MANDATORY)

Take the cleaning solution to the staff nurse.

Ask the staff nurse to verify it.

Clearly state:

Solution name

Expiration date

Bottle condition (intact / not expired)

Wait for approval before proceeding.

5. Bring Trolley to Patient Area

Move the trolley carefully to the patient bedside.

Do not touch sterile items unnecessarily.

6. Clean the Wound

Clean the wound from center to periphery.

Use one gauze for one wipe only.

Discard gauze after each wipe.

Never clean from outside toward the wound.

7. Dispose Contaminated Materials

Dispose used gauze and waste properly.

Perform hand washing after completion.

EVALUATION CHECKPOINTS (For Agents)

✔ Hand washing performed twice
✔ Cleaning solution verified with staff nurse
✔ Correct cleaning direction (center → periphery)
✔ Waste disposed properly

KEY RULES

Hand hygiene is essential.

Staff nurse verification is compulsory.

Always clean from clean area to dirty area.

Never assume a solution is safe without checking.
